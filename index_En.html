About me

Proud graduate in business administration from HEC Montreal, I never really worked as an administrator because, as rewarding as it may be, that formation didn't allow me to work in the video game industry. To reach that goal, I left Montreal to graduate from a second bachelor in video game design at Univerity of Quebec in Chicoutimi. As I was learning to code from scratch, I acquired new skills completely different, but complimentary to what I've learned in montreal. By my involvement in all aspects of student activities, I really used my skills for teamwork and my learnings in administration. I enjoy all types of entertainment may it be video games (naturally), board games, movies and books. I love to share this passion as you can realize by having a look at my contribution as a writer at Multijoueur (french only).
